Start Here
Follow these steps in order. Each one builds on the last. Takes about 20 minutes to set up completely.
1
Enter your name
Go to Settings → Your Profile → enter your first name → Save Profile.
2
Enter your debts
Go to Debt Payoff and enter every debt you have. For each one you need:
  • Debt name (e.g. "Barclay Credit Card")
  • Current balance owed
  • Interest rate (APR) — find it on your statement
  • Minimum monthly payment
💡 Tip: Enter debts in avalanche order — highest APR first. This saves the most money in interest. Your order: Avant → Barclay → Amex → SoFi.
3
Set up your recurring bills
Go to Bills and enter all recurring monthly bills. This includes:
  • Mortgage / rent
  • Utilities (electric, gas, water, internet)
  • Insurance (car, health)
  • Subscriptions (streaming, apps, gym)
  • Car payment
  • Debt minimum payments
💡 Tip: Mark everything you can as Auto-Pay = Yes. One missed payment drops your credit score 60-100 points.
4
Enter this month's budget
Go to Budget. Use the Quick-Set buttons at the top to pre-fill your income based on how many paychecks you're getting this month, then:
  • Confirm the income amounts are correct for this specific month
  • Enter your planned spending for each expense category
  • As the month progresses, fill in the Actual column
  • At month end, hit "Save Month" to record it to history
💡 Your income patterns: 4-TP month = $18,900 · 5-TP month = $21,405 · 5-TP + XE 3rd check = $23,340. Each month you navigate to that month and enter its specific income.
5
Enter your assets & liabilities
Go to Net Worth and enter what you own and what you owe. You need:
  • Assets: Home value, car values, checking/savings balances, 401(k)/IRA balances, investment accounts
  • Liabilities: Mortgage balance, car loans, all the debts from Step 2, student loans
💡 Tip: Use Zillow for your home estimate. Check each account online for current balances. Round to the nearest hundred is fine.
6
Set your savings goals
Go to Goals and set 1–3 targets. Examples:
  • Emergency Fund — target $15,000 (3 months of expenses)
  • Home Renovation — target $50,000
  • Debt-Free Date — use the What-If calculator to set a payoff date goal
7
Check your Dashboard
Go to Dashboard. Everything from steps 1–6 should now be reflected. You'll see your total debt, monthly cashflow, net worth, and savings goals all in one place.
💡 After setup, your weekly routine: Log expenses in the Expenses tab as they happen. At month end, fill in Budget actuals → hit Save Month → log any debt payments → done.
🎉
Setup complete!
